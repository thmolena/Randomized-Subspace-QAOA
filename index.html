Repeated objectives on one graph share a local observable-sensitivity geometry. The leading left singular vectors of \(J(\theta)^{\mathsf T}L\) optimally retain expected local gradient energy for a declared task-weight distribution. 01 / Problem

Why a task distribution?

Repeated objectives share observable sensitivity

Multi-angle QAOA assigns one phase parameter to each cost term and one mixer parameter to each qubit, so the search dimension grows as \[ d=p\bigl(|E|+|V|\bigr). \] On one graph, repeated tasks can change only the edge-weight vector \(w\). Their local gradients are \(J(\theta)^{\mathsf T}w\), so a declared distribution over weights identifies which parameter directions recur.

If \(M=\mathbb E[ww^{\mathsf T}]=LL^{\mathsf T}\), the leading left singular vectors of \(J^{\mathsf T}L\) minimize expected discarded squared local-gradient energy: \[ \mathbb E\|(I-QQ^{\mathsf T})J^{\mathsf T}w\|^2 =\|(I-QQ^{\mathsf T})J^{\mathsf T}L\|_F^2. \] This statement is local. It does not guarantee optimizer convergence, transfer to a new task distribution, or operational savings.

03 / Method

Training-bank geometry

Estimate, transfer, and falsify

Independent training weights estimate \(L\). Matrix-free actions approximate \(A=J^{\mathsf T}L\) by \(QB\); no evaluation weight enters construction. Full and reduced SPSA receive the same 40-step schedule. Setup, refresh, residual checks, scalar objectives, vector observables, physical shots and simulator-only VJPs remain separate in the ledger.

  1. 01

    Train

    Estimate the task second moment from a training-only weight bank and build its local tangent representation.

  2. 02

    Transfer

    Optimize a disjoint evaluation trajectory in the retained coordinates with a matched full-space SPSA control.

  3. 03

    Audit

    Compare gated, per-task, fixed, random-refresh and random-basis controls and count every resource category.

Exact local statement

What the task-weighted representation optimizes

For a fixed anchor and rank \(r\), the leading singular subspace solves \[ \min_{\operatorname{rank}(Q)=r} \mathbb E_w\|(I-QQ^{\mathsf T})\nabla C_w(\theta)\|_2^2. \] The proof is a trace identity followed by the Eckart–Young–Mirsky theorem. It selects a representation; it does not establish that a reduced optimizer reaches the same solution or uses fewer resources.

04 / Boundaries

Physical accounting boundary

Reuse measurements only where physics permits

Commuting observables

One batch, many weights—at one circuit point

Every MaxCut edge observable is diagonal in the computational basis. One bitstring batch estimates the complete edge vector and any number of scalarizations: \[ \widehat C_{w_k}=w_k^{\mathsf T}\widehat F_B. \] This saves repeated measurement only when the circuit parameters are identical. Once task optimizers follow different trajectories, their batches cannot be merged.

Break-even identity

Arithmetic is not matched-quality evidence

If full and rank-\(r\) coordinate finite differences use \(2d\) and \(2r\) circuit points per step, respectively, then reduced execution is cheaper across \(K\) tasks only when \[ K>\frac{C_{\mathrm{basis}}}{2S(d-r)}. \] This does not apply to SPSA, whose update uses two objective calls in either space. Equal shots also need not imply equal gradient precision or matched solution quality.

05 / Evidence

Released repeated-task development audit

The representation is valid; the operational gate fails

Eight graph topologies at \(n\in\{8,10\}\) and \(p\in\{1,2\}\) yield 16 configured exact-statevector cells. Each cell uses independent eight-weight training and evaluation trajectories. Full and reduced methods share the same 40-step SPSA schedule; the analysis reports descriptive paired summaries and does not treat the reused depth cells as independent graph samples.

Trace every value. The cards below summarize the committed artifact. Audit the exact row-level CSV, machine-readable summary, and paper protocol together.

Exact matched audit

Residual-gated reuse misses the quality condition

Full SPSA attains mean approximation ratio \(0.8244\pm0.0230\); residual-gated reuse attains \(0.8087\pm0.0237\). The paired change is \(-1.57\pm1.04\) percentage points. Per-task refresh is closer at \(-1.08\pm0.94\) points, but neither is a fresh confirmatory equivalence result.

Physical boundary

Compression does not reduce SPSA calls

Gated reuse averages 789.25 forward circuit points versus 648 for full SPSA, a \(1.218\times\) cost ratio, and uses an additional 138.88 simulator-only VJPs. It triggers on 89.3% of task transitions. A learned-basis advantage over a random basis is not refresh- or construction-cost matched and cannot establish an operational advantage.

Hybrid 256-shot pilot

A directionally positive mean remains unresolved

On eight \(p=2\) graph topologies, gated reuse changes quality by \(-0.33\pm1.67\) points and per-task refresh by \(+0.61\pm1.57\) points. Only scalar-objective calls use shots; construction and final scoring remain exact and simulator-assisted. These results are not hardware evidence.

Earlier fixed-objective development evidence

Retained for provenance, not used as the primary result

The following benchmark predates the repeated-task formulation. It is retained because its row-level records remain part of the public evidence chain; its parameter-compression values do not imply savings.

Depth one

Compression with a small paired quality change

Full multi-angle QAOA optimizes 23.7 parameters on average and attains approximation ratio \(0.862\pm0.053\). Two-sided RSQ at tolerance \(10^{-2}\) optimizes 14.7 parameters, a 39.1% mean cluster-level reduction, and attains \(0.867\pm0.051\). The paired change is \(+0.48\pm0.94\) percentage points, reported as mean plus or minus two standard errors across 14 topology clusters. Forward-only rank-8 RSQ compresses by 64.7% and changes quality by \(-6.39\pm1.98\) points.

Depth two

A measured failure boundary

At depth two, the average ambient dimension grows to 47.4 while RSQ retains 14.7 directions, a 69.6% reduction. The paired quality change becomes \(-4.77\pm2.47\) percentage points. The loss occurs across the tested families and rules out a blanket quality claim. Forward-only rank-8 RSQ compresses by 82.3% with a \(-14.73\pm2.65\)-point change.

Cost accounting

Measured operator cost, without a query-advantage claim

Instrumented counters separate forward evaluations, Jacobian-vector products, and vector-Jacobian products. On these small exact simulators, subspace construction and refresh require more operator applications than direct full-space optimization. At the primary tolerance, two-sided RSQ averages 749/134/481 forward/JVP/VJP actions at depth one and 867/155/556 at depth two. The categories overlap and are not physical-shot totals. The supported result is controlled parameter compression, not query advantage. All 324 of 324 scheduled checks triggered rebuilding, so this grid establishes no empirical saving from the gate. For this fixed weighted objective, one VJP returns the scalar gradient directly; multi-weight amortization is untested. Full-space SPSA also costs two objective evaluations per step, and no such baseline is included for the forward-only comparison.

Selected rank

Residual-controlled active dimension

Tightening the tested tolerance from \(10^{-1}\) to \(10^{-2}\) leaves mean rank unchanged while quality differs modestly: RSQ retains 14.7 directions on average, approximately the observable count. The residual identifies the algebraic ceiling rather than additional singular-value decay, while the ambient dimension doubles with depth. Five of 144 builds at tolerance \(10^{-2}\) and three of 144 at \(10^{-1}\) reached the ceiling before the sampled indicator met its requested tolerance.

06 / Package

Install, inspect, extend

A reusable numerical core with an auditable research artifact

The repository root is the single installable source for the rsqaoa project. The distribution contains the statevector simulator, backend-neutral sensitivity adapter, randomized factorization, repeated-task optimizers, shared-bitstring scalarization, physical resource accounting, baselines, and console command. Experiment scripts, released results, design-only confirmatory plan, paper sources, and generated figures remain checkout-only research artifacts rather than hidden wheel payloads.

Install from Git now

python -m pip install \
  "rsqaoa @ git+https://github.com/thmolena/Randomized-Subspace-QAOA.git"

rsqaoa-experiment --family ring --n 4 --p 1 --steps 1 --json

For a frozen environment, replace the default branch with a release tag or commit SHA. Bare pip install rsqaoa requires a future, separately authenticated package-index release and is not claimed here.
